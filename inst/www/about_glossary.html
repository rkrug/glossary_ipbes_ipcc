About: IPBES and IPCC Glossary Explorer

This explorer provides an interactive interface to navigate and compare terminology across the glossaries of the Intergovernmental Science Policy Platform on Biodiversity and Ecosystem Services and the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change. It brings together definitions from both bodies to support improved consistency, transparency, and cross domain understanding at the science policy interface.

The explorer was conceptualized by Aidin Niamir at the Senckenberg Biodiversity and Climate Research Centre in his capacity as Co Chair of the IPCC Task Group on Data and Head of the IPBES Technical Support Unit on Data and Knowledge Management. It is developed and maintained by Rainer M. Krug at the Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics.

The underlying data are derived from the official IPBES and IPCC glossaries. This tool is an independent, non official resource and does not represent the views, positions, or endorsement of IPBES or IPCC.

The content is provided for exploratory and analytical purposes only. While care has been taken to ensure accuracy, no guarantee is made regarding completeness or correctness. For authoritative definitions and formal use, users should refer to the original IPBES and IPCC glossary sources.
